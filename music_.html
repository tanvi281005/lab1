Guitar

The guitar is a fretted musical instrument that typically has six strings. It is often played with fingers or a pick to produce harmony and melody.

Cello

The cello is a bowed string instrument of the violin family. It is known for its deep, rich tone and is essential in orchestras and chamber music.

Violin

The violin is the smallest and highest-pitched instrument of the string family. Its versatile sound ranges from sweet and lyrical to bright and fiery.

Piano

The piano is a large keyboard instrument where strings are struck by hammers, producing a wide dynamic range and expressive musical possibilities.

Trumpet

The trumpet is a brass instrument known for its bright, powerful sound. It has been used in classical and jazz genres for centuries.

Drum Set

A drum set is a collection of percussion instruments, typically including bass drum, snare, toms, hi-hat, and cymbals, forming the rhythmic backbone of many music styles.

Harp

The harp is a plucked string instrument featuring a triangular frame and numerous strings of varying lengths. Renowned for its ethereal, resonant sound, it provides both harmonic accompaniment and soloistic flourishes.

Flute

The flute is a woodwind instrument that produces sound when air is blown across an opening. Typically made of metal or wood, it has a clear, bright timbre and an agile range spanning over three octaves.
